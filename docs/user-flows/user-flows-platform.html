Entry & user type

Everyone lands on the same front end; role and session determine what appears next.

Routing logic: Guests see Login + Sign Up. Signed-in users see username, Logout, Team, Player Profile, and Admin (only if role === 'admin'). The flows below assume the three personas you defined.

Guest — no account · not on a team

Read-only discovery and rules; auth controls promote Sign Up / Login. Team and Player nav links stay hidden until a session exists.

User with account · part of a team

After login, Team and Player Profile appear. Captains drive tournament registration; everyone sees status as registrations move through pending → approved.

Admin

Staff accounts use the same login surface but unlock the Admin panel. They do not use My Team or player competition UIs—those routes show a redirect message.
