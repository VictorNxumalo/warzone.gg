# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-14

This Privacy Policy explains how WARZONE.GG ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information when users access our platform.

## 1. Information We Collect

- Account data: email address, username, password hash, optional WhatsApp number.
- Profile and gameplay data: in-game name, team membership, participation history, match stats.
- Platform activity: registrations, announcements viewed, team invites, join requests, notifications.
- Technical data: IP address, request metadata, basic logs required for security and abuse prevention.

## 2. Why We Use Data

- To create and manage accounts.
- To operate tournaments, teams, and match workflows.
- To secure the platform (authentication, anti-abuse, rate limiting, monitoring).
- To communicate important platform and competition updates.
- To improve reliability and user experience.

## 3. Legal Bases (for GDPR-style frameworks)

- Contract performance (providing account and tournament services).
- Legitimate interests (security, abuse prevention, service improvement).
- Consent (where optional communication channels are used).
- Legal obligation (if required by law).

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data.
We may share data with essential processors (e.g., hosting, database, infrastructure providers) strictly for service delivery and security.

## 5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as needed to provide services, maintain security records, resolve disputes, and comply with legal obligations.

## 6. Security Measures

- Server-side access controls and authentication.
- Input validation on backend endpoints.
- Rate limiting and abuse detection.
- Secret management using server environment variables.
- Restricted use of privileged database credentials.

## 7. User Rights

- Access to their personal data.
