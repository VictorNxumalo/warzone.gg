Platform Lifecycle

1. Access
Guest Browses Platform

User views tournaments, schedule, bracket, and rules before registering.

2. Identity
Registration and Sign In

User account is created and authenticated for role-aware platform access.

3. Team State
Join or Build a Team

Players either join existing squads or captains create/manage team rosters.

4. Competition
Tournament Registration

Captains submit team entries, admins approve, and fixtures become visible in hub views.

5. Resolution
Admin Result Upload + Engine Processing

Submitted match results are validated and resolved by game engines to update standings, bracket progression, and public views.
