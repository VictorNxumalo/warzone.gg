Competition standards

May 2026

These sections apply to everyone who competes on EVOLVE—captains and rostered players alike—once your team is approved for an event.

Jump to: Eligibility Match format Fair play Rosters Prizes Technical Disputes
01 Eligibility
  • 1.1All participants must have an active game account in good standing for the title they are competing in.
  • 1.2Players must be 16 years of age or older. Players under 18 require written parental consent submitted before the tournament.
  • 1.3Accounts under active publisher, platform, or league bans are disqualified. Teams that field banned players may be removed from the bracket.
  • 1.4Each player may only represent one registered team per tournament season unless staff explicitly authorize a transfer.
  • 1.5Participants must meet residency or region requirements stated on the event (for example South Africa or other designated regions at registration).
  • 1.6Roster emails and in-game IDs must match what staff approved; attempting to play on unlinked or false identities is grounds for forfeit.
02 Match format
  • 2.1Series length (for example Best of 3 through semis, Best of 5 in the final) follows the announcement for that specific event.
  • 2.2Maps, modes, arenas, tracks, or stages are played according to the official ruleset published for the tournament.
  • 2.3Teams have a 10-minute grace period from the scheduled match time to be present in the lobby unless the event specifies otherwise. Missing that window can forfeit a map.
  • 2.4Private lobbies or sessions are created by the higher-seeded side unless the ruleset states otherwise. Passwords and join details are shared only through official staff or captain channels for that event.
  • 2.5Approved technical restarts are replayed exactly as directed by tournament admins.
03 Fair play & conduct
  • 3.1Zero tolerance for cheating, hacking, modded clients, macros, aim-assist exploits, or unauthorized third-party software—disqualification and bans may apply.
  • 3.2Toxic behaviour, hate speech, harassment of players or admins, or deliberate unsportsmanlike conduct can result in match forfeit or longer bans depending on severity.
  • 3.3Match results and evidence are handled as instructed by tournament staff (for example screenshots or lobby codes shared within the deadline they publish).
  • 3.4Match collusion (deliberate losing, score manipulation) is prohibited and treated as cheating.
04 Rosters & substitutions
  • 4.1Roster lock timing (for example 48 hours before start) is announced per event; changes after lock are not allowed unless staff authorize an emergency swap.
  • 4.2Platform roster cap is 6 players. Full squads cannot add a seventh player; join requests and invites are blocked at cap.
  • 4.3A team may temporarily be below six players after an approved leave/transfer event. Captains must refill open slots before any event entry that requires a full roster.
  • 4.4Players who leave enter a 7-day transfer cooldown before joining another roster (invite accept or join-request accept).
  • 4.5Substitute slots follow the registration form: mark substitutes clearly and respect the maximum substitute count for that tournament.
  • 4.6Substitutions happen between maps, not mid-map, and only after notifying an admin when the ruleset requires it.
05 Prizes & payments
  • 5.1Prize payouts are made after tournament conclusion and completion of admin verification, within the window communicated for that event.
  • 5.2Recipients must provide valid payout details as requested (for example a South African bank account where applicable).
  • 5.3Prize winnings may be subject to tax per local regulations; EVOLVE is not responsible for individual tax filings.
  • 5.4Disqualified teams forfeit prizes regardless of earlier match results.
06 Technical issues
  • 6.1Players maintain their own connection and hardware; ordinary lag or disconnects do not automatically grant a rematch.
  • 6.2Server-side crashes very early in a map may be replayed when admins confirm the scenario; keep screenshots.
  • 6.3Exploits or bugs that grant unfair advantage must be reported immediately; intentional abuse is disqualification.
07 Disputes & admin decisions
  • 7.1Disputes must be raised with admins within the time window they publish (for example 30 minutes after a match).
  • 7.2Admin decisions are final; attempting to pressure staff through harassment or brigading may result in bans.
  • 7.3EVOLVE may update these standards; approved teams will be notified of material changes.

By competing on EVOLVE you agree to the audience-specific guides plus these competition standards. Questions? Discord discord.gg/warzoneza or WhatsApp your tournament admin.
