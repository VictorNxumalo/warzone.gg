Team captains

May 2026

On EVOLVE, the captain is the account that owns the team record, submits the initial roster, and is the only role that can request tournament entries for an existing squad. These rules mirror how the platform enforces access today.

C.1 Account & platform access
  • C.1.1After sign-in you get Team and Player navigation when your profile is linked to competition data.
  • C.1.2Staff users signed in with an admin role cannot register a squad or click “Register team” on tournaments; use the Admin tools only.
  • C.1.3Only the captain account may update team identity and roster details from My Team once staff have approved your team.
C.2 First-time team registration
  • C.2.1Use Register to file a new squad: team name, tag, region, WhatsApp contact, and every roster slot with in-game name, player ID, role, device, and email.
  • C.2.2While a signup is pending staff review, the site blocks a second brand-new team application from the same captain account.
  • C.2.3Substitute designation follows the roles you assign (for example marking a slot as substitute); keep substitutes inside the roster limits described under Competition standards → Rosters.
C.3 Entering tournaments (existing team)
  • C.3.1On Tournaments, the Register team action only appears when you are signed in as a non-admin captain, your team record resolves on the platform, the event status is open, your squad is not already entered, and the event is not full.
  • C.3.2Submitting an entry queues admin approval again; players see “pending” messaging until staff accept the request.
  • C.3.3After approval, your squad’s card highlights participation and links to the tournament hub (approved teams list and fixtures).
  • C.3.4Captains may also use the Register page’s captain panel to request entry into a specific open tournament when the platform routes you there for an existing team.
  • C.3.5Save Tournament lineups on My Team (ordered presets of your six roster players). Once you have at least one preset, the captain registration form and the Tournaments grid require you to pick which lineup applies to each new entry.
C.4 Roster emails & activation
  • C.4.1Players activate their login using the same email you listed on the roster so the platform can link their account to the correct player row.
  • C.4.2Typos delay linking; double-check addresses before you submit.
C.5 Captaincy transfer & roster governance
  • C.5.1Use Transfer captaincy on My Team to hand leadership to a linked teammate. The new captain becomes the only captain authority for squad actions.
  • C.5.2Captains cannot leave a squad until captaincy is transferred to another eligible roster player.
  • C.5.3Captains can invite only teamless activated players; inviting players already in another team is blocked.
  • C.5.4Captains can accept or decline incoming player join requests. Acceptance is blocked if your roster is full.
  • C.5.5Platform roster cap is 6 players. Full squads cannot receive additional joins until a slot opens.
