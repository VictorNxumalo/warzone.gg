Result Processing Pipeline

Step 1: Input validation

Controllers verify tournament, match, team linkage, and score payload completeness.

Step 2: Resolver execution

Game-specific engine functions compute winner state and scoring artifacts.

Step 3: Persistence and progression

Services persist outputs, refresh standings, and trigger bracket progression updates.

Step 4: View synchronization

Schedule, bracket, leaderboard, and tournament hub pages show the updated official state.
