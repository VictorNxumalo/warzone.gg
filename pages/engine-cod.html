Call of Duty Engine

Built to resolve multi-map and mode-dependent COD fixtures consistently, while supporting tournament-grade auditability.

Why It Was Created

  • - COD formats are not single-score friendly; they are often best-of map series with mode context.
  • - Manual winner handling introduces inconsistencies for map count, tie-break logic, and match state.
  • - The engine creates one deterministic rule-set for all COD tournaments across admin actions and public views.

Core Responsibilities

  • - Resolve per-map outcomes and aggregate them into official match winner output.
  • - Support mode-aware logic and series progression for COD tournament formats.
  • - Provide reliable data for standings and bracket progression services.
