FIFA Engine

Built for table-driven competition where goals, wins, and points must be translated into accurate standings.

Why It Was Created

  • - FIFA leagues and group stages require standing metrics, not only match winner flags.
  • - Goal-based tie-breakers and points systems are too error-prone when handled manually.
  • - The engine standardizes aggregation and ranking logic for fair, repeatable outcomes.

Core Responsibilities

  • - Resolve scorelines into official results and winner/draw states.
  • - Aggregate cumulative team performance into standings-ready records.
  • - Calculate ordering signals used by leaderboard and qualification logic.
