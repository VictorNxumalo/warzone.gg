Public access & getting started

May 2026

You do not need an account to explore the site. This page describes what you can do as a visitor and how eligibility works once you decide to compete.

P.1 What you can browse
  • P.1.1View the tournament list, filters by game and status, prize hints, and quick match previews without signing in.
  • P.1.2Open the bracket, leaderboard, and schedule pages to follow events you care about.
  • P.1.3Read all rules sections (public, captains, squad players, and competition standards) before you commit to a roster.
P.2 When you need an account
  • P.2.1Creating a team application, joining the roster as a linked player, or opening your personal tournament hub requires sign-in.
  • P.2.2From an open tournament card, use Register or Create account to start the captain-led flow on the registration page.
  • P.2.3Staff accounts used only to run events are not participant accounts; they do not register squads or appear in brackets as players.
P.3 Eligibility basics
  • P.3.1Competition is team-based: a verified captain submits the squad and, for each new event, requests entry while registration is open and slots remain.
  • P.3.2Every player on the submitted roster must meet the age, account standing, region, and one-team-per-event expectations described under Competition standards → Eligibility.
  • P.3.3Nothing on the public site guarantees a slot until staff approve your team’s registration for that tournament.
