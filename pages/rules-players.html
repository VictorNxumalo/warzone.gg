Squad players (roster members)

May 2026

If you are on a captain’s submitted roster, you are a squad player for eligibility purposes. You compete with your team, but tournament paperwork and entries are handled by the captain account on this platform.

S.1 Account & activation
  • S.1.1Use Activate your account with the email your captain entered on the roster so the system can attach your login to the correct player slot.
  • S.1.2After sign-in, open Player to see your in-game name, role, device, squad, and stats when staff have approved your team’s registration.
  • S.1.3While your team’s latest signup is still pending, the site may limit full profile details—watch for admin approval before expecting complete stats views.
S.2 Tournament entries
  • S.2.1Individual players cannot file a brand-new team registration while already rostered under another captain; the Register flow is reserved for captains or new squads.
  • S.2.2Ask your captain to submit tournament requests from Tournaments (or the captain panel on Register) for each open event you plan to play.
  • S.2.3After staff approve your squad for an event, your tournament card shows Your squad and you can open the tournament hub for fixtures and the approved team list.
S.3 Team changes & leaving
  • S.3.1Full roster management and captain notifications live on My Team; if you are a standard roster member, use Player for your personal view instead of expecting captain tools.
  • S.3.2Leaving is blocked when competition locks apply (for example active scheduled/live matches or active approved tournament windows), and captains must transfer captaincy before they can leave.
  • S.3.3After leaving, a 7-day cooldown applies before you can join another squad, accept a team invite, or register a new squad.
  • S.3.4Leaving can open a roster slot. Teams may temporarily sit below six players and recruit again before their next event registration window.
S.4 Joining teams as a free agent
  • S.4.1Use Find squad to browse teams with open slots only (full 6-player squads are excluded).
  • S.4.2You can send join requests only while teamless, activated, and outside cooldown. Captains approve or decline requests from their governance panel.
  • S.4.3If you receive a direct invite from a captain, invite acceptance still follows the same teamless + cooldown + roster-cap checks.
S.5 Conduct & competition
  • S.5.1Every player is personally responsible for fair play, account integrity, and showing up for scheduled matches—see Competition standards for the full policy set.
