FGC Engine

Built for fighting game sets and bracket-sensitive outcomes where match structure can change finals behavior.

Why It Was Created

  • - Fighting game tournaments rely on set logic (best-of-N rounds) rather than simple one-score records.
  • - Grand-finals structures can require custom handling that differs from normal bracket rounds.
  • - The engine ensures bracket correctness when advancing winners through elimination trees.

Core Responsibilities

  • - Resolve set-level outcomes into official winner outputs.
  • - Handle edge rules around finals and progression across bracket rounds.
  • - Feed normalized results into bracket progression and public match history.
