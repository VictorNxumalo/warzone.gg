Platform Guide / Team Captains

Team Captain Journey

Captains are responsible for team ownership, roster health, and official tournament participation.

Interaction Flow

1. Team creation

Captain creates a squad, defines identity (name/tag/region), and becomes owner.

2. Roster governance

Captain manages invites, approves requests, and maintains eligible roster size.

3. Tournament registration

Captain selects tournament, submits registration, and tracks approval state.

4. Competition visibility

Approved squads get tournament hub visibility with opponents and fixtures.

System Handling

  • - Team endpoints enforce captain-level permissions on roster and team settings.
  • - Registration controllers validate slot capacity and team eligibility before acceptance.
  • - Tournament hub pages are scoped to approved participations for secure visibility.
