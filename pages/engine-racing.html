Racing Engine

Built for race-based scoring where final standings derive from placement points accumulated over events.

Why It Was Created

  • - Racing outcomes are placement-driven, with each position contributing a different points value.
  • - Multi-race formats require consistent point aggregation over time, not isolated winner-only logic.
  • - The engine guarantees fair cumulative scoring for championships and ladder-style events.

Core Responsibilities

  • - Convert finish placements into points using defined scoring tables.
  • - Accumulate race outputs into stable standings for teams or drivers.
  • - Publish ranking changes to leaderboard and schedule-related interfaces.
