Platform Guide / Guest Users

Guest User Journey

Guests are unauthenticated visitors. They can explore the ecosystem, evaluate live activity, and decide when to register.

Interaction Flow

1. Landing and discovery

Guest lands on home, sees featured tournaments, activity, and game categories.

2. Public information review

Guest browses tournaments, bracket pages, schedules, and public rules pages.

3. Registration trigger

Guest chooses Register or Login from nav and starts account onboarding.

System Handling

  • - Guest state is represented by an empty local session and hidden authenticated nav actions.
  • - Protected pages and actions prompt sign-in instead of exposing team/admin operations.
  • - Public APIs supply read-only tournament, match, leaderboard, and announcement data.
